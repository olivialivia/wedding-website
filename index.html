Accommodation

If you would like to stay overnight, we can reserve a room for you at the beautiful Channels Lodge, just a 2 minutes walk from the venue.

We've got a special rate for our guests of £60 for a double room with en-suite bathroom (extra childbeds available). If you said yes to on-site accommodation, we will send you the payment details for the room at a later date. Check-out time is 11:00. Breakfast in the morning can be purchased at the on-site café.
